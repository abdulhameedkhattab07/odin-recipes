Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a very good to eat for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • spices
  • broth or milk
  • Three cups of rice grain
  • salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. take a bowl of rice and soak it in water for two to three hours. then, cut the vegetables of your choice. now in a pan add oil, onion and vegetables and saute them.
  3. now, cook the rice separately. then, add the vegetables and rice together
  4. your fried rice is ready to eat.
